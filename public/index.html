Speaking Centre

What it is, how to book, and what to prepare.

About

The Speaking Centre helps you practice speaking skills with short 1:1 sessions with Serdar Soyer. Pick an available slot, enter your details, and you’re done.

How to book

  1. Select a green available slot.
  2. Enter your name, student number, and email.
  3. If your student number is allowed, the slot is reserved.

Rules

  • One booking per student number.
  • Booked slots are locked.
  • Past dates are closed.
